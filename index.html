About

Technology design grounded in coordination and real-world constructability.

Tyler Caraffi is an RCDD and ICT designer focused on telecommunications, security, audiovisual, and connected-building systems.

His work centers on translating owner requirements, brand standards, design criteria, and field realities into coordinated low-voltage documents that support installation, operations, and long-term system performance.

Tyler has experience supporting hospitality, multifamily, mixed-use, and commercial projects, with particular emphasis on structured cabling, telecom-room planning, video surveillance, access control, Wi-Fi infrastructure, and Revit-based coordination.
