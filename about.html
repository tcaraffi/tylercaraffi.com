Profile

Design expertise with a strong BIM foundation.

Tyler Caraffi is an RCDD specializing in telecommunications infrastructure, security systems, hospitality technology, and BIM/Revit coordination.

His work focuses on translating owner requirements, brand standards, design criteria, and field realities into coordinated ICT documents that support installation, operations, and long-term performance.

One of Tyler’s strongest professional assets is his extensive Revit experience. He uses BIM not simply to place devices, but to organize information, improve interdisciplinary coordination, develop standards, and create construction-ready documentation.

His approach is practical: reduce ambiguity, resolve conflicts early, and produce drawings that contractors can use with confidence.
